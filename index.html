How It Works

Get Started in Minutes

Setting up RideFlow is quick and easy. Here's how your family gets coordinated.

1

Create Your Family

Sign in with Google, create a family group, and invite your riders with a simple invite code or QR scan.

2

Add Your Schedule

Type naturally or use voice input. Say "Pick up Emma from soccer at 3pm" and the AI creates the event.

3

Track & Communicate

Follow rides in real time, receive photo updates, and chat with your riders. Ask @AI for instant summaries.

4

Review & Relax

Get AI-powered weekly recaps, smart alerts for conflicts, and know your children are always safe.
